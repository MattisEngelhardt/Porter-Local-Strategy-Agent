# Q2 2026 Performance Review: Financial Health and Strategic Growth

_2026-05-31 · Source documents: neura_q2_board.xlsx · en_

## Bottom Line

While Q2 saw strong revenue growth (4.2 EUR M, up from 3.1 EUR M in Q1), the company's cash runway has significantly shortened to 9.0 months. Immediate action is required to secure the proposed bridge round to maintain operational stability and fund the accelerated growth trajectory.

## Critical Financial Status: Runway Shortening and Capital Needs

The most pressing concern is the rapid depletion of cash reserves. Cash levels dropped from 22.0 EUR M (Q1 2026) to 10.8 EUR M (Q2 2026), resulting in a runway of only 9.0 months. The Board must prioritize the proposed bridge round to mitigate immediate liquidity risk. (Source: neura_q2_board.xlsx, Q2_Financials)

## Accelerated Growth Signals: Revenue and M&A Pipeline

Revenue demonstrated strong upward momentum in Q2 2026, reaching 4.2 EUR M (up from 3.1 EUR M in Q1 2026), driven by the ramping of industrial pilots. Furthermore, the strategic M&A pipeline increased to 5.0 EUR M in Q2 2026, focusing on two targets within the healthcare vertical. (Source: neura_q2_board.xlsx, Q2_Financials)

## Operational Costs and Expansion Investment

The increased burn rate reflects planned operational expansion. Monthly burn increased from 1.0 EUR M in Q1 2026 to 1.2 EUR M in Q2 2026, directly attributed to the hiring of 8 new personnel. (Source: neura_q2_board.xlsx, Q2_Financials)
